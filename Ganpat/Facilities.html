Facilities


Audio Visual Equipments

Institute has facilitated number of class rooms and laboratories with Multimedia Projectors and overhead project
ors which make teaching and learning process effective, resourceful and productive.


Audio Visual Room

Institute has developed the Audio Visual Room which is well equipped with color television, VCR, computer with
multimedia kit, multimedia projectors and internet facility. There is a facility of DTH link in order to provide various
online educational programs to the students.


Faculty and Student Development Programs

Institutes organizes various faculty and student development programs like seminars, workshops ,STTP, Guest
Lectures, quiz competition, Events of Poster and Paper Presentation etc at regular intervals. The faculty of the
institute are also encouraged to participate in various training programs, refresher courses, workshops, seminars
as well as to participate in research related activities like paper presentation, publication etc.


Training and Placement Cell

AMPICS has industry interface with many reputed companies who visit the institute every year for Campus Inter
view. Training and Placement cell of the institute provides regular training, guidance and assistance to the stu
dents to get them aware about the selection procedure . So far Ampics has a good placement record on account
of academic excellence it has built up.


Industry College Interaction Cell (ICIC)

The college faculty regularly interacts with their counterpart in the industry. Experienced IT Professionals are in
vited to take lectures and to share their knowledge and experience with faculty members and students. The ICIC
takes care of coordinating such programs.


Co-curricular Activities

Every year AMPICS organizes events like Orientation Program , Fresher Party, Teacher's Day, Sports Weeks,
Cultural Week, Garba Function, Manthan Event, Annual Function and other Social Awareness programs like
Blood Donation Camp, Medical Camp, Vruksha Ropan etc. Ampics family celebrate all these events with great
joy and exuberance. All these events provide a platform to the students to bring out their latent talents and de
velop passion of unity & team work. Events related to social awareness and welfare help to inculcate ethics, so-
cial values and patriotism among students.


Transport Facility

Ganpat University provides facility of campus buses from Mehsana to reach at campus. It has also made con
tract with other private luxury buses which also provide transport facilities from cities like Gandhinagar and
Ahmedabad.
