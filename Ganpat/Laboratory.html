Computer Laboratory

Institute has eight air conditioned computer laboratories equipped with all the latest computing software and
hardware equipment which provide access to the computers with all the latest configurations. Computer Laboratories
contributes the teaching, learning and research related activities of the Institute in effective manners. Each student has been allocated a separate computer to learn the
subject effectively. To cope with the changes in the technology, the computing equipment of the laboratories are constantly being updated.

Internet Facility

The Institute has exclusive internet lab having high speed connectivity & Wifi Facility. AMPICS provides Wi-Fi internet
access to all the students and faculty members.
